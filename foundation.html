Foundation

Foundation is a liquid or powder makeup applied to the face to create an even, uniform color to the complexion, cover flaws and, sometimes, to change the natural skin tone. Some foundations also function as a moisturizer, sunscreen, astringent or base layer for more complex cosmetics.A foundation is used alone or before you apply powder on your face and neck.Every person have their different skin tone and foundation have every color for each person. When you buy a foundation you need to choose a color that matches your skin color the way you want.
